About me

Researcher, strategist, and advocate for evidence-based product decisions.

I'm a Senior UX Researcher with 15+ years of experience, currently focused on the operational and strategic layer of research — how teams scale knowledge, share criteria, and sustain quality when research becomes a distributed practice rather than a centralised function.

My work combines mixed-methods rigor with a pragmatic view of what actually moves products forward. I lead end-to-end studies, but I'm equally interested in what happens around them: how findings travel, how non-researchers are supported when they conduct their own studies, and how research operations create the conditions for autonomy without losing depth.

I'm currently structuring frameworks for research democratisation and investigating how AI can be integrated into research workflows responsibly — amplifying analysis, documentation, and synthesis without compromising critical judgment or participant care. I care about building practices that are explicit, teachable, and adaptable to the maturity of each team.
