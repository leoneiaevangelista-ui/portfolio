About me

Researcher, strategist, and advocate for evidence-based product decisions.

I'm a mixed-methods UX Researcher with 15+ years building digital products. I combine qualitative depth with quantitative rigor — interviews, usability tests, surveys, statistical analysis, and continuous experience monitoring — to surface behaviors that matter.

My approach is methodologically rigorous but pragmatically adaptive. I believe research only moves products forward when it's communicated with clarity and tied to business impact. I work best at the intersection of evidence, strategy, and action.

At Globo, I lead cross-functional research initiatives, strengthen data-driven culture, and mentor researchers. I'm interested in research operations, autonomy within teams, and how AI can amplify (not replace) human insight.
